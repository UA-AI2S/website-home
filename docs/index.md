The [Arizona Institute for AI and Society (AI2S)](https://responsibleai.arizona.edu/ai2s) is driving the University of Arizona as a national leader in responsible AI, Machine Learning, and Data Science. We unify research, support, and campus-wide expertise to solve real-world challenges and propel innovation across disciplines through our services and programs.

## AI Info Sessions

AI Info Sessions - is designed to help the University of Arizona and the local community build confidence, fluency, and shared understanding around responsible artificial intelligence. Each virtual session is intended to spark curiosity, create a common language, and bring clarity to responsible AI and what “AI at work” looks like.
